About Us

Richard G. Agbonisa

Owner & Founder

Richard started Pater ni Richard with the vision of bringing delicious, affordable, and high-quality pater meals to the people of Iligan City. With a passion for serving the community and delivering the best food, Richard has made it a mission to provide meals that everyone can enjoy, along with great service.

Welcome to Pater ni Richard! We serve delicious and affordable Pater meals with free water, perfect for any time of the day. Our meals are prepared fresh daily, ensuring that every bite is packed with authentic flavors that will satisfy your cravings.

At Pater ni Richard, we take pride in our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and giving back to the community. Whether you're enjoying a quick meal or ordering for a gathering, we guarantee that you will have an exceptional dining experience with us.
